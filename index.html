Ultimate Peace

Ultimate Peace is a non-profit organization fostering community through the joy of throwing a disc. UP offers camps and year round programs geared to bring kids from different backgrounds together through the sport of Ultimate.

More

UP's mission is:

"To build bridges of friendship, understanding and fun for youth from different social and cultural backgrounds around the world."

UP began fulfilling their mission in the Middle East in 2009, and has recently begun offering camps in Colombia. They have been widely recognized in Israel and internationally for making a sustainable positive difference in the communities they work with.

Donate

In ultimate, "to bid," means to lay out, to unreasonably sacrifice your body for your team and the game and ultimately something bigger than yourself. We bid because we can't get enough of that plastic, because we get a rush when we close our eyes and envision being muddy, on the ground - disc in hand.

"To bid," outside the ultimate world, means to put money down for something, put a stake in something you want enough to commit to it, something you believe in even.

When we ask you to donate to Bid for Peace, we are asking you to bid, keeping both of these definitions in mind. Bid and reach for something greater than yourself. Bid out of the love of all things ultimate. Bid because you believe and would bet money on the fact that our small but mighty sport has the power to change the world for the better - starting with the next generation in the Middle East.

Salaam : Shalom : Paz : Peace
